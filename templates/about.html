Hi, I'm Andreas

With all the discussion taking place about curated feeds, I thought it would be nice to at least have the alternative to get access to my subscribed channels in an unfiltered way.

This was the idea that triggered the creation of allmysubs.video. With this project, I try to stay as transparent, as I possibly can. The sources are open on GitHub, the app currently only requires reading permissions of your YouTube account (otherwise, the subscribed channels cannot be read).

The functionality is very limited right now: For every channel, that you have subscribed to, it reads the information of the five latest video and presents this list in chronological order. That's all.

If you like the idea, you probably have as many ideas how and what to build on this foundation, as I do. Please, join the discussion and leave some Feedback.

Thanks to my friends Defender833 and Istani for their support, ideas and early testing.

If you want to support me, consider joining the development and watch some of my videos. Thanks, Andreas
