Can You Trust AllMySubs.video?

What is happening with Your data? What can be accessed?

The key take aways for You to feel safe are:

  • AllMySubs.video only has read access to Your YouTube profile. Nothing can be changed.
  • Access to Your YouTube profile can always be revoked.
  • Same data is cached up to four hours for performance reasons, but nothing is stored permanently.

In order to proof all the points, the complete sources are available on GitHub and can be reviewed or extended by everybody.
